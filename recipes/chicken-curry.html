Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Chicken Curry is a popular dish in South Asian countries. While many other countries prepare the curry in many different ways, the south asian countries tend to prepare this dish with many spices that makes it colorful and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Termaric
  • Onions
  • Garam Masala
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Fry some onions in oil
  2. Add chicken and potatos
  3. Add salt and termaric and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add all other ingredients except garam masala
  5. Cook for several minutes
  6. Add garam masala and serve.
